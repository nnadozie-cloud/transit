About Transit

Presenting Our Pledge to Support Your Travels with Reliable Car Booking Options.


Transit is a premium car rental company in Nigeria since 2015 with it's head quarter in Port Harcourt Rivers State and branches in Lagos, Owerri, Abuja and Enugu.

At Transit, the focus is on providing a seamless and convenient car rental experience to valued customers. We offer a diverse fleet of well-maintained vehicles suitable for various purposes, be it for business, leisure, or any other need. Our commitment to quality and customer satisfaction is resolute, with a dedicated team ready to help customers choose the ideal vehicle. We recognize the importance of reliability and affordability in car rentals, and thus, offer competitive rates, flexible rental options, and exceptional customer service to ensure a smooth journey for all.

Transit stands out in the car rental industry by emphasizing safety and comfort through meticulous vehicle inspections and cleaning. Our diverse vehicle options and easy online booking process make them a convenient and customer-oriented choice for quality and affordable car rentals.
